What is Traffic Control in Raja Yoga?

Traffic Control is a powerful spiritual practice introduced by the Brahma Kumaris in Raja Yoga Meditation. It is a method of pausing several times a day (at fixed timings) to withdraw from worldly activities and reconnect the mind with the Supreme Soul through pure thoughts and soul-conscious awareness.

These short meditation breaks help in controlling the "traffic" of thoughts, reducing stress, increasing clarity, and restoring inner peace. Traffic Control Songs are played at specific timings like 3:30 AM, 5:45 AM, 7:00 AM, 10:30 AM, 12:00 PM, 5:30 PM, 7:30 PM and 9:30 PM to remind practitioners to pause and connect with the Divine.

By practicing Traffic Control daily, one can maintain spiritual stability, mental discipline, and positive vibrations throughout the day.
