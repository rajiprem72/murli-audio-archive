About Sakar Vani & Avyakt Vani

Sakar Vani refers to the divine versions spoken directly by Paramathma Shiva, the Supreme Soul, through the corporeal medium of Shri Lekhraj Kripalani (Brahma Baba). From 1936 to 1969, Shiv Baba entered Brahma Baba and delivered the teachings of Raja Yoga Meditation, seated in the subtle region of his forehead. These Murlis are known as Sakar Murli and are read from Monday to Saturday.

Avyakt Vani began from 1969 onwards, after Brahma Baba became avyakt (angelic). From 1969 to 2017, Paramathma Shiva along with Avyakt Brahma Baba descended in the subtle-trance state of Dadi Gulzar. These divine messages, called Avyakt Murli, are read on every Sunday and contain subtle, angelic guidance for effort-makers and world servers.

This library provides complete access to both Sakar Murli and Avyakt Vani audio files in 23 languages, for the years 2019 to 2025.
